Shirley Temple

A Shirley Temple is pretty, pink, and irresistibly fizzy. It turns any occasion into a celebration, and itâ€™s the perfect beverage for every age.

  • Ice
  • 1 (12 ounce!) bottle Grenadine
  • 1 (2 liter!) bottle lemon-lime soda
  • Maraschino cherries, for garnish
Steps
  1. Fill glass with ice.
  2. Add 1 ounce grenadine.
  3. Top with 8 ounces lemon-lime soda.
  4. Garnish with maraschino cherries, if desired.
